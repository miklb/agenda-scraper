File No. NCA25-16111 Resolution approving Contract 25-X-01628 Sulphur Springs Pool Restoration Phase 1 between the City of Tampa and On Track Life Solutions, Inc. for the provision of Sulphur Springs Pool Investigation and Stabilization in the estimated amount of $1,833,700 for use by the Parks and Recreation Department.
    Background

    The Parks and Recreation Department has emergency need to contract with On Track Life Solutions, Inc. to investigate and stabilize the Sulphur Springs Pool. Sulphur Springs Pool is being undermined by the adjacent seawall failure through extensive soil exfiltration via large spalling delamination into the river. The Sulphur Springs Pool is a valuable City asset that is currently empty and at risk of further damage without prompt attention and currently poses liability exposure as an attractive nuisance. Due to the proximity of the spring well structure, delays in repairing the site could result in full failure of the pool and shoreline, which could pose a hazard to the well system. The well system serves as a source of water and any disruption to its ability to operate could prevent the city from meeting its obligations with other agencies and lead to environmental and resident service impacts.

    On Track Life Solutions Inc., is a city-certified Minority Business Enterprise (MBE/BBE) & Small Local Business Enterprise (SLBE).
